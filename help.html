How can I share large images through pixFly?

Sometimes Beaker browser crashes while uploading large images (size > 1 MB)
You can set a local folder for album and store images directly to that local folder.

Follow the steps:

Step 1: Open a album.



Step 2: Once you open an album right click on the page and select "View Source".



Step 3: Go to setting and click on "set a local folder".



Step 4: Click on set directory.



Step 5: After that follow the path where you set a directory and simply copy and paste images.



Step 6: Go to pixfly and refresh that page you will get images into that album.
